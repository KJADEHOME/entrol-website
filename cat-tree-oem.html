FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cat tree OEM & ODM services.

What is the minimum order quantity (MOQ) for custom cat trees?

Our standard MOQ is 100 pieces per design for private label (existing designs). For fully custom ODM designs, MOQ starts at 200 pieces. We offer trial orders of 50 pieces for existing molds to help you test the market. Mixed container (different designs) is accepted at 500+ total pieces.

How long does the OEM/ODM process take from start to finish?

Total timeline: 35-50 days. Sampling takes 7-10 days (plus shipping). Production lead time is 25-35 days after sample approval and deposit. Shipping adds 20-35 days to US/EU ports. We provide weekly progress photos so you can track production remotely.

Can you help with cat tree design and prototyping?

Yes! Our in-house design team (3 full-time designers) can create custom cat tree designs based on your brand positioning and target market. We provide 3D renderings for approval and physical prototypes for testing. Design fee is $200-500 per design (deductable from first order).

What materials do you use? Are they pet-safe?

We use FSC-certified particle board, natural sisal rope (6mm-12mm grade), high-density plush fabric (220g-600g), and solid wood options. All materials are pet-safe, non-toxic, and meet international safety standards including CE (Europe), CPST (USA), and REACH (EU). We can provide material test reports upon request.

Do you provide private label and packaging customization?

Yes. Complete private label services: embroidered logo on condo/perch, woven care label, custom hang tag, color box with your artwork, and shipping marks. Our packaging passes ISTA 1A drop test standards — critical for e-commerce and Amazon FBA.

What quality control measures do you have?

We operate a 6-stage quality control system: (1) Incoming material inspection, (2) In-process QC at 30%/70%/100%, (3) Pre-assembly check, (4) Finished product inspection, (5) Packaging QC, (6) Pre-shipment random inspection (AQL 1.5). Our factory is ISO 9001 certified. Third-party inspection (SGS/BV) available at buyer's cost.

Can you ship directly to Amazon FBA warehouses?

Yes. We have extensive experience shipping to Amazon FBA warehouses in US, UK, Germany, and Australia. We handle FBA labeling, palletizing, and dock appointment scheduling. DDP shipping terms available (we pay duties and deliver to your FBA address).

What are your payment terms?

Standard terms: 30% deposit via T/T (wire transfer) to start production, 70% balance before shipment. For orders above $20,000, we accept 30%/70% split. LC at sight is accepted for orders above $50,000. PayPal is available for sample payments.
