FAQ

Frequently Asked
Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ starts from 200 units per product. For cat trees, MOQ is typically 100–200 units depending on size and complexity. We also welcome test orders for new clients to evaluate quality before committing to larger volumes.

Do you offer OEM and ODM services?

Yes, both OEM and ODM are available. We support private labeling, custom colors, materials, packaging design, and logo printing. Our design team can also help develop new products from your concept sketches or reference samples.

What materials do you use for your cat trees?

We use premium materials including natural sisal rope for scratching surfaces, soft plush fabric for platforms, solid wood or reinforced particleboard for structural frames, and PVC or natural tree bark wraps. All materials comply with EU REACH and US CPSIA standards.

What is the typical lead time for production?

Standard production lead time is 25–35 days after order confirmation and deposit received. During peak seasons (Q3–Q4), we recommend placing orders 45–60 days in advance. Sample lead time is 7–10 days.

Which countries do you export to?

We export to 50+ countries worldwide, primarily across Europe (Germany, UK, France, Spain), North America (USA, Canada), Australia, Japan, and South America. We have experience with various import regulations and can assist with compliance documentation.

Can I get a sample before placing a bulk order?

Absolutely. We provide samples for quality evaluation. Sample fees depend on the product and are typically refundable against your first bulk order. We ship samples via DHL/FedEx, usually arriving within 5–7 business days.
