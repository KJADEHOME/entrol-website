About Entrol

Who We Are

Craft, Care,
Connection.

Founded in 2005, Entrol (Weihai Yuanchuang Import & Export Co., Ltd.) is a professional manufacturer and exporter based in Weihai, China — a coastal city renowned for its craft heritage and proximity to major shipping ports.

We specialize in premium pet products — cat trees, pet apparel, and bedding — and export to retailers, brands, and distributors across Europe, the Americas, Brazil, India, and beyond.

Beyond pet, our portfolio spans home décor, LED lighting, MDF gifts, bath accessories, and socks — giving us a broad manufacturing base and deep supply chain expertise that benefits every product category we touch.

2005

Entrol Founded

Established in Weihai with a 15-person professional team and initial focus on home décor exports.

2013

Pet Division Launched

Expanded into pet products — cat trees and pet bedding — driven by growing global demand from European clients.

2017

Factory Expansion

Own production facility expanded to 1,000 sqm. Self-inspection and QA departments established.

2018

Major Brand Partnerships

Established long-term OEM relationships with EU and US retail brands. 300+ sqm warehouse added.

2019–2020

Global Scaling

Added apparel production line. Expanded sales to Brazil, India, and South-East Asian markets.
